Classes of All Levels

Beginners

Students learn the basic steps, called adavus, and movements of the dance. These elements are the building blocks for the subsequent and more advanced sequences of dance.

Intermediates

Students learn more advanced steps and complicated patterns of movement. They also learn the names of facial expression, which are a distinctive feature of Bharatanatyam.

Advance

Students learn dance compositions that make up the repertorie of a full Bharatanatyam recital. The advanced students perform on stage at various times during the year to gain experience.

Adults

We also offer late evening and weekend classes that cater to the working adults and to dancers of all levels. Inquire for details about location and timings.
