Bharatha Arangam Dance Institute

A school devoted to the extensive training in the Kalakshetra style of Bharatanatyam

Bharatha Arangam Dance Institute is a well recognized dance institution in Warren NJ , known for its creativity, classicism and quality, Founded by Premini Thanan, a highly regarded Bharatanatyam artist.

A motivated dance teacher and an inspired choreographer. She has been teaching bharatanatyam in New York, New Jersey area for over 20 years and has earned the title “Natiya Kala Vithahar”. A lifelong student of dance, Premini started learning Bharatanatyam at the age of 4, under Kala Bhushanam Smt. Leela Selvaraja, (Disciple of Rukmini Devi Arundale who was a dancer and choreographer of the Indian classical dance form of Bharatanatyam and who laid the foundations of Kalakshetra) and continued her training with Natya Kalaimani Smt. Malini Suresh, Disciple of renowned maestro Erampu Suppiah, who founded the Kalabhavanam Dance School in Kokuvil, Jaffna, Sri Lanka. A lead dancer & choreographer in numerous dance performances and dance dramas, Premini won many international dance competitions.
